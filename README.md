# 🌍 Delhi Air Pollution Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of Delhi air pollution using **Python, Pandas, SQL (SQLite), and Power BI**. The objective is to analyze air quality trends, identify pollution patterns across cities and seasons, and develop an interactive dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and preprocess air pollution data.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Analyze pollutant trends across cities, months, and seasons.
- Write SQL queries to extract business insights.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Provide actionable recommendations based on the analysis.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Winter recorded the highest pollution levels among all seasons.
- Ghaziabad and Delhi consistently showed the highest average AQI.
- PM2.5 is one of the strongest contributors to poor air quality.
- Monsoon season recorded the lowest pollution levels due to rainfall.
- Pollution levels increase significantly during the post-monsoon and winter months.

---

## ✅ Recommendations

- Strengthen emission control during winter.
- Promote public transportation in highly polluted cities.
- Increase continuous air quality monitoring.
- Expand green cover in urban areas.
- Implement stricter industrial emission policies.
